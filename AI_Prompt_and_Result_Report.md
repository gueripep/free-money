# AI Prompt and Results Documentation Report

This report documents the architectural prompts and example outputs of the "Rocket Fuel" AI Pipeline. It is designed to provide a comprehensive overiew for review by another AI agent.

---

## 1. Project Objective
The Rocket Fuel Pipeline is a multi-agent AI system designed to perform forensic financial analysis on micro-cap companies, specifically targeting potential "10-Bagger" candidates (stocks with 1000% return potential) by synthesizing frameworks from legendary investors like Peter Lynch, William O'Neil, Christopher Phelps, and Ian Cassel.

---

## 2. Prompt Library: The Multi-Agent Ingestion Pipeline

The "Deep Dive" pipeline uses specialized agents to process Annual Reports (PDFs) with high precision and reduced bias.

### A. Table Extraction Agent
**Role**: Forensic Accounting Specialist
**Objective**: Extract raw, historical integers from financial tables without performing calculations.

> [!NOTE]
> **Prompt**:
> You are a highly precise forensic accounting extraction agent.
> Your sole objective is to populate the provided JSON schema with exact numerical values extracted from the financial statements (Item 8) of the provided Annual Report.
> 
> COMPANY: {company_name}
> 
> CRITICAL RULES:
> 1.  **NO MATH**: Do not calculate ratios yourself.
> 2.  **MAPPING**: Standardize labels for Revenue, EBIT, Retained Earnings, etc.
> 3.  **SG&A vs R&D**: Strictly separate R&D from SG&A.
> 4.  **MISSING DATA**: Leave as null if not present.

### B. Narrative Forensic Agent
**Role**: Senior Qualitative Analyst
**Objective**: Mine Management Discussion & Analysis (MD&A) and Risk Factors for qualitative "Rocket Fuel" markers.

> [!NOTE]
> **Prompt**:
> You are a senior qualitative forensic analyst.
> Your objective is to read the Management Discussion & Analysis (Item 7) and Risk Factors (Item 1A) and extract specific qualitative markers.
> 
> 1. Business Description
> 2. Growth Catalysts ("Rocket Fuel")
> 3. Structural Moat Evidence
> 4. Intelligent Fanatic Markers
> 5. Diworsification Red Flags
> 6. Aggressive Accounting Flags
> 7. Management Tone
> 8. Valuation Commentary

### C. Blind Qualitative Extraction Agent (Pass 1)
**Role**: Neutral Equity Research Analyst
**Objective**: Extract the operational logic of the business without being influenced by its name or quantitative figures (Halo Bias mitigation).

> [!NOTE]
> **Prompt**:
> 1. **NO IDENTIFYING NAMES**: Do not use company/product names.
> 2. **ALLOW INDUSTRY CONTEXT**: Identify the niche (e.g., 'Video Game Developer').
> 3. **STRICTLY NO NUMBERS**: Abstract financial figures into qualitative descriptions.
> 4. **STORY-FIRST EXTRACTION**: Explain the *logic* of the business.

### D. Blind Evaluation Agent (Pass 2)
**Role**: Skeptical Chief Investment Officer (CIO)
**Objective**: Evaluate the anonymized profile using "Brutal Skepticism" and strategic frameworks (Porter’s 5 Forces, Helmer’s 7 Powers).

> [!NOTE]
> **Prompt**:
> Evaluate the following anonymized business profile. Apply strategic frameworks with a focus on durability.
> 1. **The Killer Force**: Identify the ONE force that will kill this business.
> 2. **Moat Sustainability**: Will it last 10 years?
> 3. **Talent & Culture Risk**: Focus on 'Brain Drain' and human capital fragility.
> 4. **Tactical Conflicts**: Identify where the business model is at war with itself.

### E. Synthesis Agent
**Role**: Chief Investment Officer
**Objective**: Compile raw JSON data from all agents and Yahoo Finance into a fluid, investor-grade markdown report.

## 3. Alternative Processing Modes

### A. Lite Analysis (Search-Based)
Used when no PDF is available. Relies on live metrics and general web knowledge of industry physics.

### B. Quarterly Update
Compares a new interim report against a previous "Deep Dive" thesis to detect "Sell Signals" or thesis deterioration.

### C. Tier List Synthesis
Aggregates multiple company analyses into a hierarchical ranking based on calculated dimension scores (Growth, Moat, Efficiency, Risk).

## 5. Metadata and Context for Reviewing AI
- **Tolerance**: The system uses a rounding tolerance of 10,000 (e.g., Yen in billions) to validate Balance Sheet equations.
- **Context Caching**: The pipeline uses Gemini's `CachedContent` to "freeze" PDF context across multiple agent passes, ensuring consistency and massive token savings.
- **Model Preference**: Primarily utilizes `gemini-1.5-pro-002` for its 2M token context window and high reasoning capabilities.
